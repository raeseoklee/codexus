# Code of Conduct

[한국어](docs/ko/community/CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md)

## Our Standard

Codexus contributors are expected to keep discussion technical, specific, and
respectful. Good contributions can be direct and critical, but should stay
focused on evidence, tradeoffs, and the work.

Examples of expected behavior:

- explain technical disagreements with concrete reasoning,
- keep feedback actionable,
- respect different experience levels and contexts,
- avoid personal attacks, harassment, and discriminatory language.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- insults, threats, or sustained disruptive comments,
- sexualized language or imagery,
- harassment in public or private channels,
- publishing private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, issues, pull requests, or other
contributions that violate this code. Serious or repeated violations can result
in temporary or permanent bans from project spaces.

Report conduct concerns to the maintainer through GitHub. Security-sensitive
reports should follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
